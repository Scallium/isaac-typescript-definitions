Enumeration TrinketType

For EntityType.ENTITY_PICKUP (5), PickupVariant.PICKUP_TRINKET (350)

This is the sub-type of a trinket.

Note that the vanilla enum also has values of TRINKET_GOLDEN_FLAG and TRINKET_ID_MASK, but IsaacScript represents these as constants instead since they are not actually trinket types.
